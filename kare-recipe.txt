What will you need:

- kare cubes
- potato
- carrot
- onion
- brocolis
- butter

Peel and slice the potato and the carrot. Put them all together with the brocolis in a pan full o water on medium fire. Let it boil the wait for around 10 minutes.

Take all the vegetables out of the water but don't throw it way. Keep it.

In a large pan, but a spoon of butter with the onions sliced in cubes. Let it fry until the onions get slightly brown.

Put the separated vegetables on the pan and let them fry a little.

Finally, put the kare cubes and add small amounts of the water you boiled with the vegetables. Keep stiring until you get a thick sauce.

Bon Appetit!
